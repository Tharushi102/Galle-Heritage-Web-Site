Old Dutch Hospital

The Galle Old Dutch Hospital, located within the Galle Dutch Fort in Galle, Sri Lanka, is a historic building that has been transformed into a vibrant shopping and dining precinct. It is one of the notable landmarks within the fort and a popular destination for tourists and locals alike.

The Old Dutch Hospital was originally constructed by the Dutch in the 18th century during their colonial rule. It served as a hospital to provide medical care for Dutch officers and seafarers. The building features a blend of Dutch colonial and Sri Lankan architectural styles, with its distinctive arched colonnades and white-washed walls.

In recent years, the Old Dutch Hospital has been renovated and repurposed to house a range of shops, boutiques, restaurants, cafes, and other establishments. The restoration aimed to preserve the historical integrity of the building while creating a modern space for leisure and entertainment.

Today, visitors to the Galle Old Dutch Hospital can explore its various offerings. The ground floor hosts a variety of shops and boutiques, showcasing locally made handicrafts, jewelry, clothing, and souvenirs. You can find unique pieces of art, textiles, and other items that reflect Sri Lanka's rich cultural heritage.

The courtyard of the Old Dutch Hospital is a charming space, often adorned with colorful umbrellas, where visitors can relax and enjoy a cup of coffee or a meal. The precinct is home to several restaurants and cafes, offering a range of cuisines, including Sri Lankan, Asian, and international dishes. It's a delightful place to savor delicious food while soaking in the historical ambiance.

The Old Dutch Hospital also occasionally hosts cultural events, art exhibitions, and live performances, adding to the lively atmosphere of the precinct.

The Galle Old Dutch Hospital serves as a prime example of adaptive reuse, where a historic building has been successfully repurposed for contemporary use. It combines the allure of the past with modern amenities, creating a unique experience for visitors and contributing to the overall charm of Galle's Old Town.
