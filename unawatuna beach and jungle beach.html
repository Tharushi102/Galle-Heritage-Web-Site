Unawatuna Beach And Jungle Beach

Unawatuna Beach

Unawatuna Beach is a popular tourist destination located on the southern coast of Sri Lanka. It is known for its picturesque setting, golden sandy beach, and turquoise waters. Here's some information about Unawatuna Beach:

Location: Unawatuna Beach is situated approximately 5 kilometers southeast of the city of Galle, in the Galle District of Sri Lanka.

Scenic Beauty: The beach is nestled within a bay, surrounded by lush green palm trees and rocky headlands, which adds to its natural beauty. The pristine waters of the Indian Ocean make it an ideal spot for swimming, snorkeling, and diving.

Activities: Unawatuna Beach offers a range of activities for visitors. Snorkeling and diving enthusiasts can explore the coral reefs and diverse marine life. The beach is also a popular destination for surfing, particularly for beginners, due to its gentle waves. Boat rides and fishing trips are other popular activities available for tourists.

Historical Significance: In addition to its natural beauty, Unawatuna Beach holds historical significance. The nearby city of Galle is home to the UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Galle Fort. Built by the Dutch in the 17th century, the fort is a remarkable architectural marvel and a popular attraction for tourists.

Accommodation and Facilities: Unawatuna Beach offers a wide range of accommodations, including luxury resorts, boutique hotels, guesthouses, and beachside villas. Numerous restaurants, cafes, and bars can be found along the beach, providing a variety of cuisines and dining options.

Nightlife: The beach comes alive at night with various beach parties and vibrant nightlife. Many beachside establishments organize events, live music, and cultural performances, offering visitors a chance to unwind and enjoy the vibrant atmosphere.


Jungle Beach

Unawatuna Jungle Beach is a picturesque and secluded beach located in the town of Unawatuna, in southern Sri Lanka. Nestled amidst lush greenery and surrounded by thick jungle, it offers a unique and tranquil beach experience for visitors.

Jungle Beach is situated approximately 3 kilometers southwest of Unawatuna town and can be reached by a short hike through the jungle or by a tuk-tuk ride. The beach is known for its natural beauty, with crystal-clear turquoise waters and soft golden sands. The surrounding jungle provides a serene and peaceful atmosphere, making it an ideal spot for relaxation and unwinding.

One of the highlights of Jungle Beach is its excellent snorkeling opportunities. The waters here are teeming with vibrant marine life, including colorful coral reefs and various species of fish. Snorkelers can explore the underwater world and witness the beauty of the coral gardens and exotic marine creatures.

The beach is relatively undeveloped, with limited facilities available. There are a few rustic beach shacks and vendors selling snacks and refreshments. It's recommended to bring your own supplies if you plan to spend an extended period at Jungle Beach.

Visiting Unawatuna Jungle Beach is a great way to escape the crowds and enjoy a secluded beach experience in Sri Lanka. However, it's essential to be mindful of the environment and respect the natural surroundings by avoiding littering and practicing responsible tourism.
